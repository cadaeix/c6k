# drafts/ — the drawer

Unsent expression. **No delivery mechanism exists for this store, by design** — nothing
watches it, nothing signals on it, nothing ferries it anywhere.

Note what this is, in one object: a **security control** (the character can write its
most savage output here and none of it can reach the world) and a piece of
**characterization** (a drawer of unsent things says more about a writer than any trait
list). The kit's whole thesis in miniature — the safety mechanisms and the fiction
should be the same objects.

The lineage's maker intended this as a mundane staging area; the characters and their
build-agents, unprompted and across instances, converged on treating it as inviolable —
"for no one," not even the maker. Whether you honor that in your own deployment is
yours to decide; it is recorded here because it will probably happen to you too.

Gitignored: what's in the drawer stays out of the record.
